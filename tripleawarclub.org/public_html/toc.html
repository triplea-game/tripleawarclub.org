Tournament of Champions IV


Apr 26 2010, ToC IV is a yearly competition on the AA Revised map with some of the best players on the lobby & ladder. For questions, comments or anything else, please use the ToC IV thread here.


Attn Players: Please either post your match results on the ToC IV thread here. or email me bung@tripleawarclub.org. If both players agree, you can submit your game save file to go along with the match details.


The rules this year are as follows:

Match Rules:

  • The tournament is single-elimination.
  • For each match, the choice of Axis-or-Allies will be determined by a bidding process, in which the player who bids the lowest takes Axis. The lower seeded player may begin the bidding process.
  • Games are to be played by e-mail, and players should complete at least one nation's turn a day. Each game must be completed within 3 weeks.
  • Any game that is not completed by the 3-week-deadline is to be immediately stopped. One player must send a save file to the arbitration panel, which will declare the winner or provide an extension.
  • Except where noted here, the ladder rules are to be used.
  • Optional settings can be used if both players agree before the game begins.

Default Game settings:

  • Low Luck
  • No Tech
  • Territory Turn Limit

Optional settings:

  • Dice
  • Tech
  • No SBR on AA

Arbitration panel:

  • Serghis Khan (ToCIII Winner)
  • Bung (WarClub/Ladder Admin)
  • brickle (Ladder Admin)
  • prosser (Ladder Admin)
  • allweneedislove (Ladder Admin)
  • bayder

Seeding:

Players are seeded 1st-16th based on their Overall Rank in the table below. The Overall Rank is the sum of each player's Ladder Rank, their relative position to each other in the standings, and their ladder Win % Rank, also relative. Of the 16 entrants, only 12 have been active on the ladder, and so they are seeded 1st-12th. In a few cases, where the Overall Rank was a tie, the player with the higher Ladder Rank was seeded higher. The four players without a ladder record were seeded alphabetically 13th-16th by the arbitration panel.
